We advise on hedge accounting designations, expected credit loss (ECL) modelling supported by AI-driven data analysis, fair value hierarchy assessments, and the accounting for complex debt and equity instruments — convertible notes, warrants, and structured products.

New Standards Implementation — IFRS 18 & Emerging Requirements

IFRS 18 (effective 1 January 2027) introduces new income and expense classification, defined P&L subtotals, and mandatory disclosures on management-defined performance measures. We combine technical expertise with AI-powered impact assessment tools to evaluate effects, redesign presentation, and prepare stakeholder communications.

08
🔗
Group Consolidation & Intercompany Reporting

We provide end-to-end consolidation support for multi-entity, multi-currency group structures — covering intercompany eliminations, minority interest calculations, equity accounting, and group reporting packages. Our AI-assisted consolidation checks surface mismatches and errors before they reach your auditors, compressing your group close cycle and improving accuracy across the entire reporting chain.

09
🌉
Bridge Reporting & Variance Analysis

We design and deliver structured bridge reports that explain movements in revenue, margin, cost, and working capital between periods — actual vs budget, actual vs prior year, and constant currency bridges. Presented in a format your board and investors can act on, our bridge reporting turns complex financial movements into a clear narrative, supported by AI-assisted variance decomposition that identifies the true drivers behind the numbers.

10
📉
Derivatives & Hedge Accounting — IFRS 9 / ASC 815

Derivatives and hedging arrangements are among the most technically demanding areas of accounting — with designation, documentation, and effectiveness testing requirements that many finance teams find difficult to sustain in practice. We advise on the full hedge accounting lifecycle: identifying qualifying hedging relationships, preparing formal hedge designation documentation, applying prospective and retrospective effectiveness testing, and accounting for hedge de-designations and discontinuations. Our coverage spans fair value hedges, cash flow hedges, and net investment hedges under both IFRS 9 and ASC 815 — including interest rate swaps, cross-currency swaps, forward contracts, options, and commodity derivatives. We also assist with the transition from IAS 39 legacy hedge accounting and the presentation and disclosure requirements under IFRS 7.

We will not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

  • Legal Obligation — where processing is required to comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience and analyse performance. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

  • Essential Cookies — strictly necessary for the website to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with our website. Data is aggregated and anonymised where possible.
  • Functional Cookies — used to remember your preferences and provide enhanced features.
  • Marketing Cookies — we currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies.

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time using the cookie banner on this website, or by adjusting your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

  • Professional Advisers — lawyers, accountants, or insurers in the course of professional services they provide to us
  • Legal Authorities — where disclosure is required by applicable law, court order, or regulatory authority
